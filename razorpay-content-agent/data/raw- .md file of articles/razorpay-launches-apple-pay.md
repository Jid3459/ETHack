Indian brands are going global like never before. From fashion and luxury to SaaS and digital services, businesses from India are reaching customers in New York, London, Singapore, and beyond. But with global growth comes higher expectations. Shoppers overseas want the same seamless checkout experience they enjoy from the world’s biggest brands.

They want payments that feel fast, secure, and effortless. They want trust built into every tap. And Indian businesses, to compete on the global stage, need to deliver nothing less.

That’s why we’re delighted to announce that Razorpay has become the **first Indian payment aggregator** to launch **Apple Pay for International Payments**. Indian businesses selling abroad can offer their global customers one of the world’s most trusted, most loved, and most seamless payment methods – directly on their Razorpay checkout.

## **Why Apple Pay will redefine international payments for your business**

### **1. Frictionless Experience for Global Buyers**

Every checkout drop-off hurts. A customer finds your product, adds it to their cart, but abandons when the payment experience feels clunky or untrustworthy. Apple Pay solves this with 1-tap authentication using Face ID or Touch ID, making payments faster than ever.

### **2. Built-in Trust, Loved Worldwide**

Apple Pay is used by hundreds of millions of iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch users across the globe. For your customers, seeing the familiar Apple Pay button builds instant confidence in your brand.

### **3. Higher Conversions, Lower Drop-offs**

Merchants worldwide have reported conversion uplifts of up to 58% when enabling Apple Pay. Merchants who’ve gone live on Razorpay already are reporting success rates in excess of 90%. A smoother, more secure payment experience directly translates to higher revenue.

### **4. Global Coverage with Razorpay’s International Payments**

Apple Pay is now part of our [International Payments suite](https://razorpay.com/accept-international-payments/), alongside global card payments (Visa, Mastercard, AmEx, and Diners) and [international bank transfers](https://razorpay.com/accept-international-payments/bank-transfers/). Whether your customer is in New York, London, or Singapore, you can accept payments with the methods they trust most.

## **How to Enable Apple Pay on Razorpay**

1.   **If you’re on our Standard Checkout**

*   Fill out the form below to register your interest.
*   Then, we’ll guide you through a simple activation process.
*   Apple Pay will then be enabled on your Razorpay International Payments account.
*   Your customers will automatically see the Apple Pay button when they’re on an Apple device.

1.   **If you’re using an e-commerce platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento etc.)**

*   Fill out the form below to register your interest.
*   We’ll guide you through the necessary steps.
*   Apple Pay will then be enabled on your Razorpay International Payments account.
*   Your customers will automatically see the Apple Pay button when they’re on an Apple device.

1.   **If you’re using Custom/S2S libraries**
