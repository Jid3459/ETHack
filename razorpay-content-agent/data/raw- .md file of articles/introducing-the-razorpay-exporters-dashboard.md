If you’re an exporter, you know how nerve-wracking it can be to wait for an international payment to land.

You check your bank account. Then your dashboard. Then your inbox, hoping for some sign that the funds are on their way.

That uncertainty is exactly what the new **Razorpay MoneySaver Exporter Dashboard** aims to solve.

Built specially for exporters, freelancers, and global businesses, it gives you complete clarity on every step of your payment journey, from the moment your customer sends the money, all the way until it reaches your account.

Think of it as your control centre for global growth:

*   See how much you’re saving compared to banks and other platforms
*   Track incoming payments live 
*   Upload invoices with one click 
*   Guides to link to e-commerce and freelancer marketplaces

This isn’t just an upgrade. It’s a redesigned experience built around how exporters actually work.

## **Why We Built Our New Exporter Dashboard**

Exporters have so far managed their global transactions on our unified merchant dashboard. It was dependable, but we knew they needed more: a dashboard designed around the realities of cross-border business.

For example, tracking an international payment isn’t like tracking a domestic one. It often passes through intermediary banks, carries different reference numbers, and can take days to reconcile. Add to that the complexity of invoices and compliance documents, and exporters were spending more time managing operations than growing their business.

So we asked ourselves a simple question:

“What would a dashboard look like if it truly understood exporters?”

The result is the **new MoneySaver Exporter Dashboard**, built to make international payments as effortless as domestic ones.

## **The Impact: What It Means for Exporters**

Every new feature in the MoneySaver Dashboard is built to make your global collections **simpler, faster, and more transparent**.

Here’s what it means for you:

✅ **Stay in control of your payments******Track every international transaction in real time, from when your buyer initiates it to when it lands in your account. Every milestone, fully visible.

✅ **Save more with every transfer******The built-in savings calculator shows exactly how much you retain compared to banks and other providers, no hidden charges, no FX surprises.

✅ **Focus on growing globally******Whether you sell through Amazon, Upwork, or your own site, manage payments, invoices, and integrations, all inside one dashboard.

## **Built for Borderless Business**

The new MoneySaver Exporter Dashboard is more than a redesign, it’s a re-imagination of how exporters experience payments.
