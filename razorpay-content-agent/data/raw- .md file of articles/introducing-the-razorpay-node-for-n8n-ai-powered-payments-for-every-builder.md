In a world where anyone can build with AI and no-code, payments too can now be automated as easily as drag & drop.

Every day, small businesses, founders, and creators are building incredible things — automations that manage orders, route leads, and talk to customers. But when it comes to connecting those workflows to payments, most builders hit a wall.

That ends today.

### **Meet the Razorpay Node for n8n**

We’re excited to announce the launch of the **Razorpay Node for n8n**, a collaboration that makes it effortless for anyone — from developers to non-developers — to **monetise their workflows** without writing a single line of code.

With this integration, you can now embed Razorpay’s powerful payment capabilities directly inside n8n’s visual automation builder — bringing payments, confirmations, and reconciliation into your existing flows in minutes.

You can access upto 12+ operations with the Razorpay Node for n8n such as payment links, refunds, settlements and more! And 28+ operations with our model-context protocol.

### **Why this matters**

Automation has redefined how businesses operate. From chatbots that take orders to CRMs that nurture leads, workflows now power the backbone of growth.

But monetisation has often required developer time, backend APIs, and complex integrations. The **Razorpay Node for n8n** changes that — giving you the ability to collect, confirm, and track payments _as part of your automation_, not outside it.

Whether you’re running a home business, a D2C brand, or scaling a SaaS — now, your ideas can monetise themselves.

**What you can build**

With the Razorpay Node, you can create end-to-end monetisation flows — visually and intuitively.

 Some popular use cases include:

*   **Chat-to-Order Workflows****** Automate order collection over WhatsApp or Telegram and generate Razorpay payment links instantly.
*   **Payment Insights Automations******Automate real-time payment tracking. Build a bot that gives you revenue updates, subscription stats, or refund status instantly.
*   **CRM and Dashboard Integrations****** Sync payments with Google Sheets, Notion, or Slack to monitor performance in real time.
*   **Reconciliation and Reporting****** Automate settlements, refunds, and reports to keep your books up-to-date — without manual effort.

From chat to checkout to confirmation — it all just flows.

### **How it works**

The Razorpay Node plugs directly into n8n’s drag-and-drop builder. [Start building from here](https://n8n.io/integrations/razorpay/).

Here’s how it works:

1.   **Authenticate** securely using your Razorpay API credentials.

2.   **Choose from multiple operations** like:

    *   Create Payment Link
    *   Fetch Payment Details
    *   Capture or Refund Payment

3.   **Connect seamlessly** with 400+ other apps on n8n — like WhatsApp, Slack, or Google Sheets.

### **Why Razorpay x n8n**

Both **Razorpay** and **n8n** share a common goal: empowering builders.

n8n gives you the freedom to automate anything. Razorpay gives you the infrastructure to monetise everything.

Together, we’re helping businesses of all sizes move faster — enabling founders, teams, and entrepreneurs to turn automation into action, and action into revenue.

**“Every business deserves the ability to monetise at the speed of their ideas. With the Razorpay Node for n8n, we’re putting that power directly into the hands of the builder.”************—****_Khilan Haria, Chief Product Officer, Razorpay_**

**The future of building is open**

This launch is a step toward **democratising monetisation** for every builder. We’re continuing to work with the n8n and developer community to expand capabilities — unlocking more use cases, endpoints, and intelligent automations.

If you can build it, you can now monetise it. All inside your workflow.

**The Razorpay Node for n8n. AI-powered payments for every builder.**
