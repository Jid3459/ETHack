> _We make moments special with thoughtful gifting_

**_— Pawan Gadia, Global CEO, FnP_**

That’s not just a vision – it’s the heartbeat of everything FnP does.

It all started with one bouquet. One simple, heartfelt attempt to add a touch of meaning to a gift. But in that single act of curation lay a much bigger idea: what if gifting could be more than a formality? What if it became a way to truly connect people—across time zones, generations, and borders?

And so, Ferns N Petals was born, not just as a flower shop, but as a mission to craft memories. Since 1994, FnP has grown into India’s go-to destination for celebrations. From romantic surprises and birthday hampers to “just because” gestures, FnP has been there for millions, delivering love in a box to over **100+ countries.**

But here’s the thing, gifting isn’t just about what’s in the box. It’s about how it makes someone feel. That’s why the team at FnP doesn’t just sell products; they sell moments. Thoughtfully chosen, perfectly timed, and incredibly personal.

## **When Distance Meets Delight, Razorpay Makes It Possible for FnP**

To truly become a global celebration enabler, FnP needed more than just a wide bouquet catalog; they needed the ability to collect payments seamlessly from across the world. That’s where Razorpay stepped in.

With Razorpay’s[**International Payments gateway**](https://razorpay.com/international-payment-gateway-india/), FnP unlocked a world of possibilities—powering smooth, secure cross-border transactions and making global gifting as seamless as sending flowers next door.

## **Celebrating Together, Everywhere**

From late-night surprises to long-distance love, FnP is turning every moment into something magical. And with [Razorpay](https://razorpay.com/) powering seamless global payments, those moments now travel the world with ease.

Because when the right partner joins the journey, every celebration feels closer.

Here’s to thoughtful gifting, made borderless with FnP and Razorpay.
