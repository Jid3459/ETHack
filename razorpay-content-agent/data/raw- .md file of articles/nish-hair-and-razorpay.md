> _I’m not just making hair, I’m making confidence you can wear_

_**— Parul Gulati, Founder, Nish Hair**_

It started with a single clip-in—and a dream big enough to change how women see themselves.

When Parul Gulati founded Nish Hair in 2017, she wasn’t just selling hair extensions; she was building a movement. A movement that said _you deserve to feel beautiful, strong, and unstoppable, every single day_. No more bad hair days. No more compromises. Just limitless confidence, one strand at a time.

Born out of pure love—for hair, for craftsmanship, for self-belief—Nish Hair became a pioneer in DIY extensions and clip-in toppers in India. But what made them truly special wasn’t just the product; it was the promise: _dream-worthy hair for every woman who dared to dream._ Today, Nish Hair has grown from a handful of clients to a global sisterhood, proving that when beauty meets empowerment, there are no borders it can’t cross.

## **Making Good Hair Days Global with Razorpay**

To truly spread the joy of “no bad hair days” across the world, Nish Hair needed a trusted partner who could keep up with their growing global dreams. That’s when [**Razorpay**](https://razorpay.com/) turned aspirations into accessible reality.

With Razorpay’s[International Payment gateway](https://razorpay.com/international-payment-gateway-india/), Nish Hair unlocked the ability to accept payments from customers worldwide — easily, securely, and without friction. Women from New York to New Delhi, London to Dubai, could now shop for their perfect hair match without any checkout hurdles. Payments became smoother, shopping became faster, and most importantly, dreams became more reachable.

Together, we’re making good hair days truly global.

## **The Confidence to Go Global**

From one woman’s vision to a worldwide movement, Nish Hair’s journey proves that when purpose meets the right partner, magic happens. With Razorpay by their side, they didn’t just scale—they soared. And as their global sisterhood grows stronger every day, one thing’s certain: bad hair days don’t stand a chance.
