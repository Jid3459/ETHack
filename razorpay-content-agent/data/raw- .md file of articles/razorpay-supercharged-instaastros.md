With **1,500+ expert astrologers**, over **4 crore call/chat minutes**, and **50 lakh+ happy customers**, InstaAstro is transforming how people access spirituality in the digital age. From connecting users with trusted astrologers, tarot readers, and spiritual consultants to offering high-quality spiritual products through its e-commerce store, [InstaAstro](https://instaastro.com/)’s mission is to provide a seamless, credible, and enriching experience.

At the heart of this journey lies an essential element—**payments**. To support its growing global audience and ensure every spiritual connection flows effortlessly, InstaAstro partnered with **Razorpay**, enabling smooth, reliable transactions across borders and platforms.![Image 3](https://d6xcmfyh68wv8.cloudfront.net/blog-content/uploads/2025/02/Quote-InstraAstro-scaled.jpg)

# **Real-Time Needs, Global Dreams: InstaAstro’s Payment Challenges Unveiled**

As InstaAstro scaled its operations, ensuring seamless payments became a top priority. This meant any payment solution needed to meet a few key requirements:

## **1. Real-Time Payments for Instant Consultations**

InstaAstro operates in the B2C category, where instant transactions are key. For users seeking consultations on the go, payments needed to be quick, reliable, and frictionless. Interruptions during live calls or chats due to wallet recharges were frustrating and could break user intent.

## **2. High Success Rates (SR) to Minimize Drop-Offs**

Payment failures were a major hurdle. For users eager to continue their spiritual consultations, any failed transaction led to a potential loss of engagement. Ensuring a high payment success rate was critical.

## **3. Global Reach, Local Challenges**

With users spanning North America, European, SEA, and the MENA region, InstaAstro needed a robust solution to process international payments efficiently. Their expansion demanded a partner that could handle diverse payment methods while maintaining reliability.

With users often seeking instant spiritual advice, any interruption due to payment failures could disrupt the experience. Razorpay’s UPI technology minimised such disruptions by enabling fast wallet recharges and seamless payment processing.
