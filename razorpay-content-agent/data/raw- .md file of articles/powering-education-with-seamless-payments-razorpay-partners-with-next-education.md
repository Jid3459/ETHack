Be it preschools, schools, an academy, or independent online tutoring, managing an educational institute is no small task. From academics to administration, they juggle multiple responsibilities every day. But for many, the **admission process**, **fee collection, and reconciliation remain some of the most time-consuming and error-prone processes**.

That’s exactly where **Next Education** is making a difference. As one of India’s leading EdTech providers, Next Education offers its cloud-first, AI-driven, digital operating system – NextOS. Built around a user-friendly, next-gen ERP, NextOS automates everything from fee collection to report generation and seamlessly integrates with a full suite of EdTech tools like attendance management, digital learning modules, holistic report cards, and proprietary publications. Their mission is simple: empower educational institutes to focus more on students and less on administrative hassles.

## **The Challenge: Fee Collections Were a Bottleneck**

Traditionally, fee management required significant manual effort. Staff needed to track transactions, reconcile records, and manage discrepancies. This not only drained resources but also created friction for parents who had limited payment options.

While Next Education’s NextOS (ERP) digitised much of this process, the **quality of the payments experience depended heavily on the payment platform powering it.** And that’s where Razorpay stepped in.

## **The Razorpay Advantage**

Today, over **100+ schools using NextOS rely on Razorpay** to power their fee collections. Educational institutes prefer Razorpay because of a number of reasons such as:

*   **Smooth, high-success transactions**: Unlike other gateways that face frequent failures, Razorpay delivers reliability and speed.
*   **Quick issue resolution**: Escalations are addressed promptly, building confidence for both institutes and parents.
*   **Flexible payment options**: Parents can pay via UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets, ensuring accessibility for everyone.
*   **Real-time reconciliation**: Institutes’ admins can track and manage payments in one dashboard without manual errors.![Image 3](https://blog.razorpay.in/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Gemini_Generated_Image_ekt5kwekt5kwekt5-scaled.png)

    *   **9 million** transactions processed, that’s how NextOS has streamlined the school’s fee collection.
    *   **89%** of educators say they save **5–10 hours every week** with NextOS.
    *   **85%** of education professionals believe NextOS is **10x better** than any other academic or administrative platform.

## **Looking Ahead: Scaling Together**

Next Education continues to expand its footprint across India, with Razorpay powering payments for schools of every size, from standalone institutions to large educational groups. Together, **the partnership ensures that****payments never become a barrier to learning or growth.**
